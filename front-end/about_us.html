About EcoTrack

EcoTrack is an innovative platform designed to help coal mining companies track, manage, and reduce their carbon emissions. With a mission to promote sustainable practices, EcoTrack leverages cutting-edge technology to visualize carbon footprints, encourage green energy adoption, and empower users with actionable insights.

Our Mission

Our mission is to drive the mining sector towards carbon neutrality by providing tools to monitor emissions, encourage reforestation efforts, and estimate solar energy adoption. Together, we can create a sustainable future.
