Welcome to EcoTrack

Welcome to EcoTrack, a platform designed to empower individuals and organizations to make a tangible impact on the environment. By planting trees and uploading geo-tagged images of your efforts, you can earn carbon credits, which can later be converted into real money once the plants mature. In addition, EcoTrack features a solar panel cost calculator to help you estimate the cost of installing solar panels on your rooftop and calculate the potential energy outputs. With EcoTrack, you can contribute to a sustainable future while earning rewards for your green actions.
