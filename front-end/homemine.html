Welcome to EcoTrack for Coal Mines

Welcome to EcoTrack for Coal Mines, a platform dedicated to helping coal mining companies track, manage, and reduce their carbon emissions. Through the submission of mining data and visualization of carbon emissions, coal mines can actively participate in reducing their environmental impact. Our platform also provides access to data insights, which can help coal mines in calculating their emissions and identifying areas for improvement.

Key Features

  • Submit Mining Data: Upload mining operations data to assess carbon footprint.
  • Carbon Emission Visualization: View real-time emissions data and analysis to understand your environmental impact.
  • Profile Management: Manage your company's profile and monitor your sustainability progress.

Our Impact

Over 50+ coal mines have joined EcoTrack to reduce their carbon emissions and work towards a greener future.
